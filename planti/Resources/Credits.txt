<p>
Planti is your dietary companion to find vegetarian and vegan restaurant menu Items around you and in any specific location. Planti also enables you to contribute new items to help many fellow vegans and vegetarians to discover more choices.
</p>
<h4>Features</h4>
<ul>
<li> Selecting your dietary preference
<ul>
<li>Vegan: Fully plant-based diet. No meat, dairy or Egg</li>
<li>Ovo-Vegetarian: Eat mostly plants in addition to eggs. No meat or dairy</li>
<li>Lacto-Vegetarian: Eat mostly plants in addition to dairy. No meat or eggs</li>
<li>LactoOvo-Vegetarian: Eat plants based along with egg and dairy. No meat</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Automatically search for restaurants with your preferred food preference in your location</li>
<li>Search in a specific address or location</li>
<li>Map view displays pins for all restaurants</li>
<li>Move map and refresh search to find in that specific location</li>
<li>List view of all restaurants and find route and navigation to a specific restaurant with default maps on the
mobile</li>
<li>Toggle between all four options in both map view and list view</li>
<li>Menu items view with photos and easy navigation between menu items in a restaurant</li>
</ul>
<br>
<h4>Contribution Option</h4>
<ul>
<li>Help vegetarians and vegans to discover more food preferences by posting new restaurants menu items and
photos</li>
<li>Add new menu items for existing restaurants</li>
<li>Add photos for menu items</li>
</ul>
<br>
<h4>Location Permission</h4>
<p>Planti app uses user's location to find the restaurants in their area. We will never store location details in
any form</p>
<h4>Camera Permission</h4>
<p>Planti app asks users to give permission to use device camera while user contributes new menu items.
</p>
<h4>Feedback</h4>
<p>Please share any feedback or questions you may have by sending email to team@plantiapp.com</p>
