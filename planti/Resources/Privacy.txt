Your Privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect your information through our planti mobile app and use, As used in this Privacy Policy, "Planti", "we", "us", and "our" refer to Planti.

Information we collect and their usage

Location: We collect location information to provide restaurants and menu items of your choice in your current location. You can disable location sharing at any time in mobile settings. However, Planti provides best experience when location is turned on. We will not store your location in any form at any time.

Email: We collect user email address while posting new menu items using Planti mobile application. This is completely optional and will be used for the purpose of promotional giveaways. The email address is stored in Planti databases and may be used for sending specific emails to users related to Planti.
